Our Mission

At EduSetGo India, we are dedicated to making international education accessible and achievable for every Indian student. Our mission is to bridge the gap between ambitious students and world-class universities, providing comprehensive guidance and support throughout the entire journey.

We believe that every student deserves the opportunity to pursue their dreams, regardless of their background. Through personalized counseling, expert guidance, and unwavering support, we transform the complex process of studying abroad into a smooth and exciting adventure.

Excellence

We strive for excellence in every aspect of our service, ensuring the highest quality guidance for our students.

Integrity

We maintain the highest standards of honesty and transparency in all our dealings with students and partners.

Commitment

Your success is our success. We are committed to supporting you every step of the way.

Our Policies

Transparent, ethical, and student-focused approach to education consulting

Privacy Policy

We respect and protect the privacy of our students. All personal information shared with us is kept strictly confidential and is used only for the purpose of facilitating your education abroad journey. We never share your data with third parties without your explicit consent.

Refund Policy

We offer a fair and transparent refund policy. If you're not satisfied with our services within the first 30 days, we provide a full refund. For visa-related services, refunds are processed according to the specific terms agreed upon at the time of enrollment.

Code of Ethics

We adhere to the highest ethical standards in education consulting. We provide honest advice, accurate information, and never make false promises. Our recommendations are based solely on what's best for the student's academic and career goals.

Quality Assurance

We maintain rigorous quality standards in all our services. Our counselors undergo regular training, and we continuously update our knowledge base to provide the most current and accurate information about universities, programs, and visa requirements.

Student Support

Our commitment doesn't end with admission. We provide ongoing support throughout your education journey, including pre-departure briefings, arrival assistance, and continued guidance during your studies abroad.

Partnership Ethics

We partner only with accredited and recognized educational institutions. All our university partnerships are transparent, and we never compromise on quality for commercial gains. Student welfare always comes first.
