USA Student Visa (F-1)

Official Info

Requirements

  • Valid passport (6+ months validity)
  • Form I-20 from SEVP-approved school
  • SEVIS fee payment receipt
  • DS-160 form completion
  • Visa interview appointment
  • Financial proof ($40,000+ recommended)
  • Academic transcripts
  • English proficiency test scores

Processing Time

Interview Wait Time: 2-8 weeks

Processing After Interview: 3-5 business days

Peak Season: May-August (longer waits)

Fees

Visa Application Fee: $185

SEVIS Fee: $350

Total: $535

Pro Tips for USA Visa

Interview Preparation

Practice common questions about your study plans, career goals, and ties to your home country.

Financial Documentation

Show consistent bank statements for 6+ months and avoid large, unexplained deposits.

Academic Records

Ensure all transcripts are officially translated and authenticated if not in English.

UK Student Visa (Tier 4)

Official Info

Requirements

  • Valid passport
  • Confirmation of Acceptance for Studies (CAS)
  • Financial proof (tuition + £1,334/month living costs)
  • English language proficiency
  • Tuberculosis test (if applicable)
  • Academic qualifications
  • Genuine student assessment

Processing Time

Standard Service: 3 weeks

Priority Service: 5 working days

Super Priority: 24 hours

Fees

Visa Application Fee: £363

Healthcare Surcharge: £624/year

Priority Service: +£500

Pro Tips for UK Visa

CAS Requirements

Apply for your CAS only after meeting all academic and English language requirements.

Financial Evidence

Maintain required funds for 28 consecutive days before application.

Biometric Appointment

Book your biometric appointment early, especially during peak seasons.

Canada Study Permit

Official Info

Requirements

  • Letter of acceptance from DLI
  • Valid passport or travel document
  • Proof of financial support (CAD $10,000+)
  • Letter of explanation
  • Medical exam (if required)
  • Police certificate
  • Digital photo
  • Language test results

Processing Time

Online Application: 4-12 weeks

Paper Application: Longer processing

Peak Season: May-September

Fees

Study Permit: CAD $150

Biometrics: CAD $85

Total: CAD $235

Pro Tips for Canada Visa

Statement of Purpose

Write a compelling letter explaining your study plans and future intentions.

Financial Planning

Show funds for first year tuition plus CAD $10,000 for living expenses.

Work Opportunities

Study permit allows 20 hours/week work during studies, full-time during breaks.

Australia Student Visa (Subclass 500)

Official Info

Requirements

  • Confirmation of Enrolment (CoE)
  • Genuine Temporary Entrant (GTE) statement
  • English language proficiency
  • Financial capacity evidence
  • Health insurance (OSHC)
  • Health examinations
  • Character requirements
  • Academic documents

Processing Time

75% of applications: 29 days

90% of applications: 4 months

Peak Season: October-February

Fees

Base Application Fee: AUD $710

Additional Applicant 18+: AUD $530

Additional Applicant <18: AUD $180

Pro Tips for Australia Visa

GTE Statement

Clearly explain your genuine intention to study temporarily in Australia.

Financial Evidence

Show AUD $21,041/year for living costs plus tuition and travel expenses.

Health Insurance

Purchase OSHC before applying and ensure it covers your entire stay.

Germany Student Visa

Official Info

Requirements

  • University admission letter
  • Completed visa application form
  • Valid passport
  • Biometric photos
  • Proof of financial resources (€11,208/year)
  • Health insurance
  • Academic certificates
  • Language proficiency proof

Processing Time

Standard Processing: 6-12 weeks

Peak Season: 3-4 months

Apply Early: 3 months before travel

Fees

Visa Fee: €75

Service Fee: Varies by country

Blocked Account: €11,208

Pro Tips for Germany Visa

Blocked Account

Open a blocked account (Sperrkonto) to prove financial resources.

Language Requirements

German programs require DSH-2 or TestDaF. English programs need IELTS/TOEFL.

Early Application

Apply 3 months before intended travel due to longer processing times.

Ireland Student Visa (Stamp 2)

Official Info

Requirements

  • Letter of acceptance from recognized institution
  • Valid passport (6+ months validity)
  • Proof of English language proficiency
  • Evidence of academic qualifications
  • Financial evidence (€7,000+ recommended)
  • Private medical insurance
  • Completed online application
  • Immigration fee payment

Processing Time

Visa-required countries: 8 weeks

Non-visa required: On arrival registration

Peak Season: July-September

Fees

Single Entry Visa: €60

Multiple Entry Visa: €100

Registration Fee: €300

Pro Tips for Ireland Visa

Work Permission

Students can work 20 hours/week during term and 40 hours during holidays.

Financial Requirements

Show €7,000 for studies under 6 months, €7,000 per year for longer courses.

Registration Process

Register with immigration within 90 days of arrival to get your IRP card.

New Zealand Student Visa

Official Info

Requirements

  • Offer of place from approved education provider
  • Valid passport
  • Evidence of funds (NZ$15,000/year minimum)
  • Completed application form
  • Medical and chest X-ray certificates
  • Police certificates
  • Academic transcripts and qualifications
  • English language proficiency (if required)

Processing Time

Online Application: 20-25 working days

Paper Application: 25-30 working days

Peak Season: October-February

Fees

Student Visa: NZ$375

Multiple Entry: NZ$590

Immigration Levy: NZ$35

Pro Tips for New Zealand Visa

Financial Evidence

Show NZ$15,000/year for living costs plus tuition fees and return airfare.

Work Rights

Full-time students can work up to 20 hours/week during studies.

Health Requirements

Medical exams required for stays over 6 months or certain countries.
