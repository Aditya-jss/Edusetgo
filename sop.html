Frequently Asked Questions

How is your SOP different from what consultancies provide?
Unlike consultancies that use templates and junior writers, our SOPs are crafted by alumni from top universities who understand what admissions committees actually look for. Each SOP is written completely from scratch based on your unique profile and story.

We specialize in turning weaknesses into strengths. Our expert writers know how to address low GPAs, employment gaps, or other challenges in a way that shows growth, learning, and future potential rather than dwelling on past shortcomings.
