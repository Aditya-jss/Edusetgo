🏛️ Types of Universities

🏛️ Public Universities

State-funded, large institutions with lower tuition fees. Examples: UCLA, University of Texas, Penn State.

🏢 Private Universities

Independent institutions with higher fees but excellent facilities. Examples: Harvard, Stanford, MIT.

🔬 Technical Institutes

Specializing in engineering and science. Examples: MIT, Cal Poly, Georgia Tech.

🎨 Liberal Arts Colleges

Focus on undergraduate arts and sciences. Examples: Amherst, Swarthmore, Williams.

🏆 Ivy League

Most prestigious institutions. Harvard, Yale, Princeton, Columbia, Cornell, Brown, Dartmouth, UPenn.

🏘️ Community Colleges

2-year institutions offering Associate degrees. Examples: Bellevue CC, De Anza College.

🎯 Academic Systems

📅 Semester System

Fall (Aug-Dec) & Spring (Jan-May). Most common system with summer sessions available.

📊 Quarter System

Fall, Winter, Spring & Summer quarters. 10-week intensive programs.

📈 Trimester System

Three 15-week terms per year. Less common but flexible scheduling.

📝 GPA System

4.0 scale grading. A=4.0, B=3.0, C=2.0, D=1.0, F=0.0 points.
