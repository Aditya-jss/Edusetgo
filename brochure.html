About EduSetGo

EduSetGo is India's premier overseas education consultancy, dedicated to transforming the dreams of Indian students into reality through world-class international education opportunities.

Founded with the vision of making quality international education accessible to every deserving student, EduSetGo has established itself as a trusted partner for thousands of students seeking to study abroad. Our comprehensive approach combines personalized counseling, expert guidance, and unwavering support throughout the entire education journey.

🎯 Our Vision

To be the most trusted bridge connecting Indian students with world-renowned universities, fostering global citizens who contribute meaningfully to society.

🌟 Our Expertise

With years of experience and partnerships with over 500+ universities across 10+ countries, we provide unmatched expertise in international education.

👥 Our Team

Our team of certified counselors, visa experts, and education consultants brings together decades of experience in international education.

🏆 Our Success

We have successfully placed over 10,000+ students in top universities worldwide with a 95% visa success rate and 100% customer satisfaction.
